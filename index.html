Hi! I'm Zi Wang, a Research Engineer at NVIDIA working on end-to-end autonomous driving algorithms and Vision-Language-Action models. I am also a voluntary researcher at NVIDIA's Generalist Embodied Agent Research (GEAR) Lab, where I focus on visual sim-to-real frameworks for humanoid robotics. I completed my Master's in Computer Vision at Carnegie Mellon University under Prof. Srinivasa Narasimhan, and my Bachelor's in Artificial Intelligence at Shanghai Jiao Tong University under Prof. Siheng Chen. My research spans autonomous driving, embodied AI, and humanoid loco-manipulation. If you're interested in discussing my work, feel free to reach me at wang.zi2865@gmail.com.
